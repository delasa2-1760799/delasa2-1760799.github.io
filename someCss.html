All About Froyo

Frozen yogurt, or froyo, is a dessert commonly eaten in the United States. In froyo shops, customers are able to dispense different yogurt flavors into a cup. The price of the yogurt is usually based on the weight of the cup.

There are many froyo shops across the nation, such as Menchie's, Yogurtland, and Tutti Frutti. One of the closest froyo shops to the UW is the Menchie's located in University Village. Frozen yogurt is a healthier option than ice cream.
